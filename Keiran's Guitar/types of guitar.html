The Acoustic Guitar is the evolution of the classical. Instead of nylon strings, the acoustic guitar now has steel strings.
The electric guitar is steel string like the acoustic, but not hollow usually. This needs to be amped up to get the right sounds.
Classical guitars are a cheap first guitar although string spacing means its difficult to start with on chord shapes.
